EXTRAS

Ryan's love of music began as a young child, playing popular music alongside his Royal Conservatory of Music classical studies throughout his primary school days.  As a teenager, Ryan joined his first alt-rock band Obscenery playing bass guitar.  As Ryan began experimenting with improvisational music on piano, he soon joined the Phish inspired Baked Ockman, playing at Toronto’s favourite jam venues including the Comfort Zone and Opera House.

When Baked Ockman band mates went their separate ways for university, Ryan responded to an ad online looking for a keyboardist for a psychedelic rock band called the Peyote Kings.  Ryan became quick friends with the band-to-be and over the next six years, the group recorded a full length album and EP and played dozens of shows across southern Ontario. 

When the group played their final show in 2006, Ryan had already joined his current band ‘The Show’, essentially a recreation of his high school jam band ‘Backed Ockman’.  The Show amassed a devoted local following and was gigging regularly but Ryan was still looking for an outlet to record his more singer-songwriter material leading him to join The Interphase Project. 

Ryan’s enthusiasm for music and recording remain as strong as ever and he is always recording new material for his various projects.
