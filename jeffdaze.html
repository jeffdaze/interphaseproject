Jeff Daze is a talented musician, songwriter, and recording artist. As a milti-instrumentalist, Jeff excelles in various musical styles. Jeff's strongest suit is a keen awareness of melody.

His involvement with the Interphase Project grew out of sharing his musical experience in a Toronto-based rock act The Peyote Kings, where he played bass.

Playing with David Hein during his Mirvish production of the musical "My Mother's Lesbian Jewish Wiccan Wedding" has definitely been one of Jeff's musical career highlights.

Jeff hails from Bellevile, Ontario, and currently calls Newmarket his home.
