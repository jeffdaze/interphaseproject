Nino Monaco Bio:

 

Ecliptic gleaming brass, held together on chrome plated steel

Swiveling pedals with mallets, the thump one can only feel

The highs in the air, pass by that tonal crackle

While the four on the floor, punch the kick unshackled

Cylindrical shine of carbon fibre, maple, birch and mahogany

Scream in concert and roar independently

Wild and feverish contained in a small number count

The man on the throne is the one to surmount

Blurred cacophony mix of metals, wood, flesh against skins

The backbone and pumping heart is the music which stems within

Years spent toiling,  primal in its craft, alert and aware

The influence of those before me, bring me there

Copeland, Peart, Rich and Bonham, just to give you some

The sound you are hearing is the beat of my drums...
